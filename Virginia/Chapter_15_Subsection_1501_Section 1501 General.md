# CHAPTER 15 ROOF ASSEMBLIES AND ROOFTOP STRUCTURES

 SECTION 1501
 GENERAL

**1501.1 Scope.**

The provisions of this chapter shall govern the design, materials, construction and quality of roof assemblies, and rooftop
_structures._
